# Team Lead - Reports

Team leads focus on department delivery, task review, workload visibility, and project execution for their team.

![Team Lead Reports](/docs/screenshots/team-lead/reports.png)

## When To Use This Page

Use this page when you need to complete reports work as the Team Lead role. The screenshot above was captured while signed in as this role, so the visible navigation and actions reflect the permissions available to that panel.

## Step By Step

1. Open Reports from the sidebar.
2. Use filters/tabs to separate drafts, submitted reports, and items awaiting review.
3. Create or open a report and complete all required fields before submission.
4. Attach supporting documents where the workflow expects evidence.
5. Submit, approve, reject, or comment according to your role permissions.
6. Confirm the report status changes after the action is saved.

## Checks Before You Leave

1. Confirm the saved state appears on screen.
2. Refresh or revisit the page if the action affects counts, dashboards, or project boards.
3. Notify the next responsible person if the workflow now depends on them.
